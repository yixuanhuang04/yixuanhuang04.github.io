Beyond Academia

Welcome to this space where I share the side of me that exists beyond research. Life, after all, is more than achievements—it is the small moments, the passions, and the quiet connections that shape who we are. Here, I hope to open a window into what makes me myself: the experiences that inspire me, and the reflections that accompany my journey.

Beyond research and academia, there are countless things that form the tapestry of my life. My academic activities matter deeply, yet it is the seemingly ordinary, unique fragments of daily life that contribute just as much to my growth and identity. I am grateful that you are willing to explore this part of me, to look past the academic façade and see a fuller picture of who I am.

Academic homepages often highlight milestones and accomplishments, but I long to record something more—the ordinary yet memorable moments that give life its meaning. These fragments may appear unrelated to academia, but for me, they are essential in shaping the person I am becoming: someone devoted not only to scholarship, but also to life itself. This section is, in a sense, a personal memoir. One day, when I look back, I hope I will not only see a list of achievements, but also the path I have walked, step by step, to reach them.

I once read a line that has never left me: “If my story can be remembered by one more person, I have lived one more life.” Perhaps, when I revisit this page years from now, I will find that I was not the most accomplished academic. But I will see someone who kept trying, who embraced curiosity, who cherished moments, who shared experiences, who enjoyed life, and who created memories that will always remain.

I love experiencing life in all its forms. Traveling brings me joy—whether walking through vibrant cities to feel the rhythm of local culture, or wandering in the countryside to discover the uniqueness of each landscape. I also find delight in learning across disciplines, from astronomy and biology to literature and the arts. These explorations not only enrich my days, but often bring unexpected sparks of inspiration that ripple back into my academic journey.

And so, I warmly invite you to join me—not only in my academic pursuits, but also in the everyday moments that shape who I am. By reading this, you have already become part of my story. Perhaps fate has brought us together, weaving our paths into something meaningful. Maybe one day we will meet at a conference, share an afternoon tea, exchange fresh ideas, and create something exciting together. Or perhaps we will simply become friends—traveling, learning, and witnessing the growth of our time side by side.

In the end, as a blessing and a promise, I leave you these words:

朝暮与年岁并往，然后一同与你行至天光。
— As dawn and dusk travel along with the years, walk with you, together into the light.
